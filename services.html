How It Works

The Five-Step Kraken Install

Diamond Grinding

Mechanical surface prep opens the concrete's pores for a permanent bond — never shortcuts, never acid etching.

Crack & Spall Repair

Every crack is chased, filled, and sealed. Moisture testing confirms the slab is ready before a drop of coating goes down.

Epoxy Base Coat

Professional-grade pigmented epoxy rolled to the correct millage — the structural backbone of the system.

Broadcast To Rejection

Flake or quartz is broadcast until the floor can't hold any more, then scraped smooth. Full, uniform coverage guaranteed.

Polyaspartic Topcoat

Crystal-clear, UV-stable topcoat locks it all in with a mirror shine. Walk on it the same day, park on it in 24 hours.
