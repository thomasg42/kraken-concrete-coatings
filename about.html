About Us

Built On Craftsmanship

Kraken Concrete Coatings is Montana's premier epoxy and flake coating specialist — founded in Bozeman, owned by the guy who still shows up on your job site.

The Founder

Meet Emanuel Parra-Montes

After years working in the concrete coating industry, Emanuel recognized the demand for high-quality, reliable coating services in Montana. Believing in his ability to deliver superior results and exceptional customer service, he founded Kraken Concrete Coatings in 2020.

His hands-on experience and commitment to craftsmanship ensure every project meets the highest standard — Emanuel personally walks every job, and his crew preps every floor like it's their own.

  • Zero shortcuts. Diamond grinding, crack repair, and moisture testing on every single job.
  • Professional-grade materials. Citadel Floors products — not big-box kits.
  • Straight answers. Honest quotes, real timelines, and a lifetime adhesion warranty.
